Design Concept

The Eye That Sees Tomorrow

The Foresight logo features a stylized eye with a rising stock chart embedded in its pupil. This symbolizes the platform's core purpose: seeing market movements before they happen.

  • The Eye — Represents vision, insight, and the ability to see what others miss
  • The Chart Line — A green upward trend showing growth and bullish predictions
  • The Prediction Point — The glowing green dot represents the future price point the AI predicts
  • Neural Nodes — Subtle data points around the eye represent AI/ML processing
  • Orange Gradient — Matches the project's primary brand color for consistency
